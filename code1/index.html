Infant Health Trends in Australia

This line graph tracks the trends of infant births and deaths in Australia from 2010 to 2021. It highlights the impact of healthcare improvements and immunization initiatives on reducing infant mortality rates. The graph is interactive, with tools for users to explore data across different regions and time frames, helping policymakers and healthcare providers gauge the effectiveness of public health interventions.
