Immunisation and Mortality Data Visualization

Using a bubble chart, this visualization explores the relationship between immunization rates and preventable mortality across various diseases. The size and color of the bubbles indicate the severity of preventable deaths and the extent of immunization coverage, respectively. This interactive tool allows users to delve into specific details about the effectiveness of immunization programs and identify areas needing improvement. description for the bar charts
