Visualization of Deaths by Year

This stacked bar chart compares infant deaths to total deaths in Australia over the same period, illustrating the proportion of infant mortality within the overall mortality rate. It provides a clear visual representation of how public health measures, especially immunization programs, have successfully decreased infant mortality rates despite a general increase in total deaths due to population growth.
